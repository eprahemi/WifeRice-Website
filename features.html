Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

QuickShell Widgets

Battery with liquid effects, volume with drag boost, network manager, Bluetooth, calendar with weather, clipboard, music player, settings panel, and more — all custom-built in QML.

Matugen Dynamic Theming

Colors extracted from your wallpaper in real time. Every popup, bar, and widget adapts to your current palette — no manual theming needed.

Amethyst Noir Palette

Deep purple, indigo, and cyan accents across all UIs. Hardcoded fallback palette ensures consistency even when wallpaper colors don't cooperate.

Floating Sidebar

A right-edge floating sidebar with quick actions, system stats, and tool launchers. Activated by edge hover — minimal, clean, accessible.

Wallpaper Picker

Browse wallpapers with color filters, video preview support, and one-click apply with smooth transitions. Images auto-sorted by dominant color.

Audio Auto-Switch

Plug in headphones or Bluetooth — audio switches automatically. Unplug? Back to speakers. Zero configuration, just works.

Lock Screen + Music

A beautiful lock screen with clock, wallpaper background, and built-in music player. Play your local MP3 collection right from the lock screen.

Auto-Update Notifier

A background daemon checks for dotfiles updates every 10 minutes. Get notified when a new version drops — update with one click.

SDDM Theme

Custom matugen-themed SDDM login screen with wallpaper background. Your desktop aesthetic starts at the login screen.
